Product Changeover

Assumptions and Prerequisite Skills
  • The unloading bay has current unloading product stuck behind loading product
  • Requires strong knowledge of how the Bessermatic works, as well as the curing process of racks regarding loading and unloading from the car
Supplies Needed
  • standard PPE
Step 1

Outside the control room, there is a chart indicating what product is in each bay. If a bay has a combination of two or more different products (ex: “2 racks 8x16, 2 racks 10x16”), there is a chance the unloading product is stuck behind the loading product.

Step 2

Before the car goes to that bay, proceed to the control panel next to the kiln door. Flip the control switch to the “manual” position.

Step 3

Turn and hold the “Kiln Door” switch to the “open” position until the door is no longer moving. Be mindful to slow the door down when nearing the back-stop to prevent slamming.

Step 4

Go to the bay with the mixed products and assess how many racks have the unloading product. Keep in mind that there may be a rack that has both loading and unloading product. This needs to be taken into consideration.
