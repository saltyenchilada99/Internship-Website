Scenario 7

Step 7.1

Wait until the crawler has loaded its first rack onto the new loading bay and has just retrieved its unloading rack. Flip the car control switch to the “Hand” position (Green circle in Figure 7.2).

Step 7.2

Use the lever on the second row to move the car to the “reverse” position (Red circle in Figure 7.3). You can press the “Manual Accel” button to speed the car up (Pink circle in Figure 7.3).

Step 7.3

hold the “Reverse” lever from step 7.2 until the car is out of the kiln room and at the furthest rail from the door (Red circle in Figure 7.4). Stop moving the car when the “Car Centered Right” button lights up in this area (Figure 7.5).

Step 7.4

Before you unlock the rail, flip the “Manual Crawler Auto” switch to the “Crawler” position (Red circle in Figure 7.6).

Step 7.5

Flip the “Right Raillocks” switch to the “Lock” position (Purple arrow in Figure 7.7) and hold it at that position until you see the “Right Locks Locked” box (Red circle in Figure 7.8).

Step 7.6

At this time, move and hold the “Crawler” lever to the “Right” position (Red circle in Figure 7.9) until the entire rack is on the storage railway.

Step 7.7

Move and hold the “Crawler Elevator” lever to the “Down” position (Red circle in Figure 7.10) until the rack is independently sitting on the rail.

Step 7.8

Move the crawler back into the car. Hold the lever until you see the “Crawler on Car” label light up (Red circles in Figure 7.11).

Step 7.9

Once the crawler comes back to the car, unlock the raillocks (Purple arrow in Figure 7.12).

Step 7.10

Take the car to the “Loader” rail and lock the right raillocks.

Step 7.11

While the car is collecting the loading rack, Press the “Program Car” button (Red circle in Figure 7.14).

Step 7.12

Press on the blue box to the right of the “Location” label until you see “Car at loader” (Yellow circle in Figure 7.15).

Step 7.13

Flip the control switch back to “Auto” and press the “Auto Pump Start” button (Purple circle in Figure 7.16).

Step 7.14

Wait until all three loading racks have been dropped off at the loader. Follow the car into the kiln room as it takes its loading rack into its proper bay.

Step 7.15

Once the loading rack has been dropped off, set it back to manual, move the car back to where the mixed-product rack is, and drop it off in the unloader rail.

Step 7.16

Repeat 7.10-7.13.

Step 7.17

Go to the kiln door control panel. Flip the control switch to “Auto” and press the “Auto Start” button (Red button in Figure 7.19.
