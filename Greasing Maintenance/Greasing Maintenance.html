Greasing Maintenance

Assumptions and Prerequisite Skills
  • The machine is fully off and lockout-tagout procedures have been followed
Supplies Needed
  • standard PPE
  • grease Gun
  • grease Canisters (For backup supply)
  • ladder
Step 1

Remove one of the grease-gun batteries from its charger and attach it to the end of the gun (Figure 1).

Step 2

Get a lithium grease pack and open up the plastic lid (Figure 2).

Step 3

To open the grease magazine, pull out the handle with force. Then, once the handle is all the way out of the gun, push down on the handle so it rests in the slit (Figure 3).

Step 4

Twist the magazine counter-clockwise to pull out the magazine. Load the grease container in the magazine with the opening facing up (Figure 4).

Step 5

Screw the magazine back in the gun. Slightly pull out the handle to unlock it and push the handle back to its original position (Figure 5).

Step 6

Attach the nose to the grease filling (Figure 6).
